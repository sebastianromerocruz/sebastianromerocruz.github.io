What is Le Cabinet Historique ?
A historical allusion. It's just the alias I use when working on projects, as a kind of motivator. The name is based on the Cabinet Historique et Topographique Militaire, a small, highly efficient organization within the war ministry of the First French Republic, where Napoleon Bonaparte briefly worked in 1795. I like French history, that's it.
